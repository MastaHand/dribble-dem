Dribbling

Learning how to dribble is really important. You can say it is the most important thing while you are playing basketball.
You cannot play the game without dribbling.You have to bounce the ball on the ground.
Make sure you dont bounce it too hard or too low.
Listed below are some ways to get better at Dribbling:

  • Dribbling low and fast
  • Dribbling while running
  • Use both hands at different time to dribble
  • Dribble without looking at the ball
